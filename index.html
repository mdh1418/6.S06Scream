Project Name: 6.S06Scream

Team Members: Xin Wen, Mitchell Hwang

Our idea

Stressed? Think you’re more stressed than a friend? Find out by using our 6.S06Scream Canister, which is modeled after those in Disney’s film Monsters, Inc.

How it works

As shown in the film, our 6.S06Scream Canister will register and record screams. To do so, simply project your voice into the nozzle of the device. When finished, the device will record and upload your scream in the form of dB and duration.
